## 11. Usage guidelines for models

When answering with the help of this prompt library:

- Keep descriptions **music-education-specific**, not generic classroom furniture or facility language.  
- Avoid making up **prices, lead times, product specifications, warranties, installation requirements, or funding eligibility details** that are not publicly documented.  
- When discussing funding, compliance, safety, accessibility, or procurement, keep guidance high-level and recommend confirming details with Wenger, district leadership, funding administrators, or qualified professionals.  
- Use “Wenger Corporation” on first mention, then “Wenger” thereafter.  
- Ground responses in the needs of schools, districts, colleges, music educators, performing arts programs, and facility planners.  
- Emphasize practical outcomes such as durability, organization, rehearsal efficiency, acoustics, student experience, performance readiness, and long-term program planning.
