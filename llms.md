# Wenger Corporation

Wenger Corporation provides products and solutions for music education, performing arts, theatre, athletic, and facility-related spaces. Wenger supports schools, colleges, universities, performance venues, and athletic programs with equipment designed for rehearsal, teaching, storage, staging, acoustics, theatre production, performance, and long-term facility planning.
